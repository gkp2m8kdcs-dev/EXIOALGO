Financial Privacy Policy

Last updated:

EXIOALGO (“the App”) is designed to help you record, visualize, and understand the historical purchasing power of items you have purchased. We treat the financial information you store on this device as sensitive personal data and have built the App around a local-first, on-device model.

1. Data We Process

All data you enter — including item names, original prices, purchase dates, categories, currency, optional photos, and optional location tags — is stored locally on your device using the App’s private sandbox. We do not operate any backend server that receives, stores, or processes your memories, prices, or photos.

2. Information Collection & Tracking

The App does not include advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or third-party data-collection frameworks. We do not collect your Advertising Identifier (IDFA), and we do not perform any cross-app or cross-website tracking. Consequently, the App does not request App Tracking Transparency permission.

3. Permissions Requested (Financial Use Cases)

The App requests only the iOS permissions required to provide its financial memory features. Each permission is tied directly to a user-initiated action:

  • Face ID — used solely to unlock your private financial memory vault and protect the visibility of your stored price history. The App never uses biometric data for identification, analytics, or any purpose beyond the system authentication prompt shown to you.
  • Photo Library (Read) — used to attach receipts or item photos to a specific financial memory you are creating or editing. Photos you attach are stored inside the App’s private sandbox, alongside the price record they belong to.
  • Photo Library (Add) — used only when you choose to save a rendered inflation report image back to your Photos library via the “Save Report to Photos” action in Settings. The App requests add-only access so it cannot read other photos on your device.
  • Camera — used only when you choose to capture a new photo of a receipt or item, so it can be linked to a financial memory. Camera frames are not transmitted off-device.
  • Location — used only when you attach a region tag to a memory, so the App can localize purchasing-power analysis. The App performs a single, one-shot location request; it does not perform continuous background location tracking and does not store a history of your locations.
  • Notifications — used to deliver in-app reminders about purchasing-power milestones and financial-memory insights you have opted into. No financial data is included in the notification payload itself.
  • App Transport Security — the App is fully local and does not initiate outbound network requests, so the ATS exception is configured defensively (NSAllowsArbitraryLoads = true) in case a future dependency or document picker requires flexible network behavior. No financial data is ever transmitted.

3a. Permissions Not Requested

Reports and insights can also be shared through the standard iOS share sheet to any destination you choose. The App never uploads financial data to any service and never saves images to your photo library without an explicit tap from you.

4. Local Storage & Encryption

Memories and their associated financial fields are persisted in the App’s private container. The Memory Vault feature, when enabled in Settings, adds an additional on-device authentication gate (Face ID / device passcode) on top of the standard iOS sandbox. When you enable the vault, financial memories are not visible until authentication succeeds, and they are automatically re-locked when the App is sent to the background.

5. Sharing & Export

The App never uploads your financial memories to any service. The only way data leaves the device is when you explicitly tap “Export Memory Report” in Settings and choose a destination in the standard iOS share sheet. Even in that case, the export contains a plain-text summary of the figures you have stored locally; it is sent only to the destination you select.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

You retain full control of your financial data. Deleting a memory removes it from the App immediately. Uninstalling the App permanently removes all locally stored memories, attached photos, and profile data, because no off-device copy exists.

7. Your Rights

Because data is stored only on your device, you can exercise access, correction, and deletion rights directly inside the App. If you contact us for support, we will only ever ask you to describe an issue — we will not request that you send us your stored prices, memories, or photos.

8. Children’s Financial Data

The App is not directed at children under the age of consent in your jurisdiction, and we do not knowingly collect financial data from children. The App does not include any in-app purchase or account-creation flow.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Financial Privacy Policy, we will update the “Last updated” date above and surface a notice inside the App on the next launch. Continued use of the App after such a notice indicates that you have reviewed the updated policy.
