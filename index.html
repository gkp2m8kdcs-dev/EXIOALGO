EXIOALGO · iOS

Privacy & Data Information

Last updated:

EXIOALGO is a personal financial-recordkeeping app that helps you record, visualize, and understand the historical purchasing power of items you have purchased. We treat the data you store on this device as sensitive and designed the App around a local-first, on-device model: no backend receives, stores, or analyzes your memories, prices, or photos.

This page is an overview. The complete legal text is in the Financial Privacy Policy.

Read the full policy →

Highlights

Local-only Data lives on-device

Every field you enter — item, price, date, category, photo, optional location — is stored inside the App's private iOS sandbox.

No No tracking

No advertising SDKs, no analytics trackers, no IDFA collection, and no cross-app or cross-website tracking.

On request Permissions only when needed

Face ID, Photos, Camera, Location, and Notifications are requested only when you take the action that needs them.

You decide Sharing & export

Data only leaves the device when you tap Export and choose a destination in the iOS share sheet.

Quick Facts

  • No account required. You can use the App without signing up for anything.
  • No in-app purchases. The App contains no payment flow that touches a third party.
  • No background tracking. No location, activity, or usage telemetry is collected in the background.
  • Optional Memory Vault. A Face ID / passcode gate in Settings hides your price history until you authenticate.
  • Uninstall = erase. Because nothing is stored off-device, removing the App permanently deletes all locally stored data.
